üìà 200-Day SMA

The 200-day Simple Moving Average is the gold standard for long-term trend analysis. When price is above the 200-day SMA, it's generally considered a bull market. When below, it's a bear market.

üîÑ Golden Cross

A Golden Cross occurs when the 50-day SMA crosses above the 200-day SMA. This is a major bullish signal that often precedes significant upward moves and is widely followed by institutional investors.

üìâ Death Cross

A Death Cross happens when the 50-day SMA crosses below the 200-day SMA. This is a major bearish signal that often precedes significant downward moves and can indicate the start of a bear market.
